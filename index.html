Search G ("we," "our," or "us") operates services that utilize third-party APIs, including the Pinterest API, to search for and display publicly available content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our services.

Information We Collect

Our application accesses the Pinterest API solely to perform searches and retrieve publicly available content such as pins, images, and descriptions. We do not collect, store, or process personal information from Pinterest users.

How We Use Information

Data retrieved through the Pinterest API is used only to display search results to our users. We do not sell, share, or distribute this data to any third parties.

Data Storage

We do not permanently store any data obtained from the Pinterest API. Search results are displayed in real time and are not retained after the session ends.

Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with third-party platforms, including Pinterest. Your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Pinterest's Privacy Policy at policy.pinterest.com.

Cookies
